Dating Analysis

Here are some broad attirbutes to think about while dating as well as discriptive words that support the category.

  1. Attraction
    • Looks
    • Maturity
    • Chivalry
    • Smile
  2. Faith
    • Active?
    • Temple
    • Worthy
    • Regular Book of Mormon Studies
    • Mission (optional)
  3. Compatibility
    • Do you relate in any way?
    • Laugh together
    • Both want to be around eachother
    • Good Comunication
    • See eye to eye
  4. Background/Family
    • Have family?
    • Hometown
    • Siblings
    • Relationship with family
    • Does his/her family get along with/like you?
  5. Aspirations
    • Want to be rich?
    • Future family
    • Future in the church
    • Go getter?
    • Doing things for the right reasons
  6. Personality Traits
    • Integrity
    • Humble
    • Confident
    • Happy
    • Peacemaker
    • Calm
    • Kind
    • Empithetic
    • Driven
    • Polite
    • Solid communicator
